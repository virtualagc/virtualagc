This page provides Grumman Apollo Lunar Module electrical and mechanical drawings from a aperture-card box 507 stored at the U.S. National Archives Southwest branch in Fort Worth, Texas.  Any given box is mostly devoted to a specific numerical drawing-number range, except for filing errors.  Besides the drawing-index on this web-page, the following related resources are available:

  • Grumman LEM engineering drawings from other aperture-card boxes.
  • MIT/IL Apollo G&N System engineering drawings, including the Apollo Guidance Computer (AGC).
  • Background information about the Apollo engineering drawings provided by the Virtual AGC Project.
In the following table, Type=DW is a "drawing", while Type=EO is an "engineering order".

Warning:  This index table is based on metadata originally punched into the aperture cards holding the microfilmed slides of the drawings.  That original punched metadata was often incorrect, as well as lacking drawing-title information!  The drawing numbers or other data listed below may therefore be inaccurate; however, wherever drawing titles are shown, then a manual correction to the metadata has subsequently taken place and the data is much more likely to be accurate.

Scans whose drawing numbers are shown in red (LDW280-12345), have been identified as having legibility problems serious enough to jeopardize recovery of information from those drawings.  In most cases the illegibility is due to the scanner settings used, and hopefully the marked drawings can rescanned in the future with different scanner settings.
